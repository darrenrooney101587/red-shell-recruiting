Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 26, 2025

Red Shell Recruiting ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the personal information we collect from users of our website, www.redshellrecruiting.com.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name
  • Contact information (such as email address)
  • Resume details (including work history, education, certifications, and skills)
  • Other information you voluntarily provide through forms or email

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Evaluate candidate qualifications
  • Match candidates with potential employment opportunities
  • Communicate with you regarding your application or profile
  • Improve our services

We will not sell your personal information to third parties.


Information Sharing

We may share your information only with:

  • Prospective employers who are seeking candidates for job opportunities
  • Service providers who assist in operating our website or providing services to you (under confidentiality agreements)

We do not share your information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.


Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the personal information we collect. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.


Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your information at any time

To make a request, please contact us at: nomad.recruitment.nyc@gmail.com


Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
